✨ Key Insights

Golden Era of Releases

The number of movies released has grown exponentially since the late 90s, peaking around 2014-2015 with nearly 2,000 top-tier releases annually.

Dominant Genres

Drama and Comedy dominate the landscape, accounting for massive portions of Hollywood's total output, but Action and Thrillers follow closely.

The Cost of Blockbusters

High budgets correlate strongly with high revenues, but massive outliers like Avatar and Star Wars redefine scale, achieving billion-dollar profits.

Audience Sentiment

Most movies cluster around a solid 6.0 to 7.0 rating from the audience. Masterpieces scraping above 8.0 are exceedingly rare but celebrated.
